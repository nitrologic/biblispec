kanuka (kunzea ericoides) vs manuka (leptospermum scoparium) — same myrtle family, different genera entirely

the practical field guide:

| | kanuka | manuka |
|---|---|---|
| vibe | tall tree, soft leaves, lives 150 yrs | shrubby, prickly, 60 yr sprint |
| flowers | tiny clusters, once yearly | big singles, irregular encores |
| bark | long leathery strips | short papery flakes |
| honey | light, AGP anti-inflammatory | dark, MGO antibacterial |
| oil | gentle, alpha-pinene | potent, antimicrobial |
