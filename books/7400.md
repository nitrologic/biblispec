## 74-165 Shift Register

```
⬜╔═1═LOAD═□═VCC 16═╗🟥
⬜║ 2 CLK    INH 15 ║⬜
⬛║ 3 E        D 14 ║⬛
⬛║ 4 F        C 13 ║⬛
⬛║ 5 G        B 12 ║⬛
⬛║ 6 H        A 11 ║⬛
⬜║ 7 _QH    SER 10 ║⬜
🟩╚═8═GND══════QH═9═╝⬜
```

3. E
4. F
5. G
6. H
7. _QH
8. GND
9. QH
10. SER
11. A

14. D
15. INH
16. VCC
